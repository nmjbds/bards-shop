Overview

Every BARDS piece is produced as a pre-order — made specifically for you once your payment is confirmed. Because production begins immediately, our refund and return conditions are strictly defined below.

We ask that you read this policy in full before completing your purchase.

02

No Returns Policy

All sales are final once payment is confirmed and production has begun. The following are not valid grounds for a return or refund.
  • Change of mind after payment
  • Incorrect size selected by the customer
  • Colour or design preference change
  • Delivery within the stated production timeline
  • Minor texture or shade variation inherent to garment production

We strongly recommend consulting our size guide before ordering. If you have any doubts, contact us before purchasing — not after.

03

When We Refund

We will issue a full refund in the following circumstances only.
  • BARDS is unable to fulfil your order for any reason
  • The item received is defective due to a manufacturing fault
  • The wrong item was shipped to you
  • A significant production delay beyond four weeks occurs without prior notice
04

Defective Items

If you receive an item with a manufacturing defect — stitching errors, printing faults, or structural issues — or receive the wrong item entirely, you are entitled to a full replacement or refund.

  • You must contact us within 48 hours of receiving the item
  • Provide your order ID and clear photographs of the defect or wrong item
  • The item must be unworn and in its original condition

Claims raised after the 48-hour window may not be eligible for resolution.
