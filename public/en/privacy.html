02

How We Use It

  • To process and fulfil your orders
  • To send order confirmations and shipping updates
  • To respond to customer support requests
  • To improve our website and product experience
  • To send promotional messages — only if you have opted in

Your data will never be used for purposes beyond those stated without your explicit consent.

03

Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. Ever.

We share data only where necessary:

  • Logistics partners — to deliver your order within Cambodia
  • Payment processors — KHQR / ABA / Bakong, for transaction processing only
  • Legal authorities — when required under Cambodian law
04

Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account remains active, or as needed to provide our services. Order records are retained for up to two years for business and legal purposes.

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us directly.

05

Cookies

We use essential cookies to maintain your session and shopping cart. We may also use lightweight analytics to understand how our site is used — no advertising cookies, no cross-site tracking.

Cookies can be disabled in your browser settings; some site functions may not work correctly without them.

06

Your Rights

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of any inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact support@bardskh.com.

07

Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational safeguards to protect your data against unauthorised access or misuse. Passwords are stored in hashed form; all site traffic is encrypted via HTTPS.

No transmission over the internet can be guaranteed 100% secure. We encourage strong, unique passwords for your BARDS account.

08

Third-Party Links

Our website may link to external platforms such as social media. BARDS is not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party sites. We recommend reviewing their policies before sharing any information.

09

Children

Our services are not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a child has provided us with their information, please contact us immediately for prompt removal.
